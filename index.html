Donte's Anime Reviews

This webpage is a list of Anime and what I would rank them. (0-100)

Naruto

About a ninja who was born with a demon fox inside him. This demon fox destroyed the village just before he was born so the village he lives in has resented him since he was born.

Rating: 70

Bleach

A boy with the ability to see spirits finds himself suddenly attacked by one. A strange woman with a sword comes to his aid. Before the final blow is struck she gives him her powers so he can slay the beast.

Rating: 86
